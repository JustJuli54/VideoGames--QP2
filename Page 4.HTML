A briefly covered statement on the home page is genres of games.
This will go better in to detail on all genres of games


Many games have more than one genres, as different genres can go hand in hand with eachother.
For example Simulation games can also be RPGs (Role-playing games), and sandbox games.
Another example is Action games they can also be survival, FPS (First-person shooter), battle royale, and strategy games.

There is a huge list of all the different genres of games. As there are genres and then there are also sub genres. Sub genres help define the game more than the main genre(s).
For example sub genres in some Action-Adventure games are Cinematic Action-Adventure, Action RPG, and Open world Action Adventure.
