VIDEO GAMES


Video games have been around for much long than you would expect. The first video game was invented in 1958, by William Higinbotham and it was called "Tennis for two". Altough the game only lasted for two years, The idea grew and became more popular in the 1970's when "Pong" by Atari was made.
With arcades starting to open throughout the 1970s, the original arcade-style game of "Pong" came out in 1972. Video games have changed a lot through out the years from the 1970's to present day, technology its self has developed and evoled into an essential item in our life's.
